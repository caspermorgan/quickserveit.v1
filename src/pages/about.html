The Founder's Journey

How it all began

After a long recovery phase in life, the founder started working as an IT staff member in a nearby intermediate college. What began as a simple job soon revealed deeper digital challenges that everyday people faced.

Teachers typing on phones

Exam papers were being prepared on mobile devices instead of proper computers.

Creators using low-end apps

Content quality was suffering because of limited video editing tools and resources.

Students running between shops

Simple tasks like printing a PDF involved visiting multiple places and wasting time.

Businesses with no digital presence

Small shops had almost zero online branding or documentation infrastructure.
